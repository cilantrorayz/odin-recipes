Fish Taco

Loved by Caroline and Gus this savory dish will make any sea food lover happy. That red cabbage maybe a pain to get but it will be worth the one year wait.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Tortilla
  • Red Cabbage
  • Mayonnaise

Steps for making!

  1. Heat up the Tortilla in a skillet with light oil on both sides.
  2. Sear the Tuna in a pan.
  3. Saute the tuna.
  4. Freshly chop the Red Cabbage.
  5. Mix the Tuna, Mayonnaise and chopped Red Cabbage.
  6. Put the fillings on the Tortilla and shape them into Tacos!
  7. Enjoy a nutritious meal full of protein, fibre and omega 3 fatty acids!
