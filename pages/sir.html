Squid Ink Ravioli


This special pasta dish allows you to traverse the depths of the caves without a worry about debuffs.

Ingredients

  • Squid Ink
  • Wheat Flour
  • Tomato

Steps for Making!

  1. Add flour and water to make the dough. Press it out into Ravioli.
  2. Crush the roasted tomato in Squid Ink to create the sauce.
  3. Boil the pasta for 4-6 mins until they float to the top.
  4. Incorporate some of the pasta water into the sauce. Mix the sauce and pasta well!
  5. Enjoy this special dish of the valley for undisturbed combat!
