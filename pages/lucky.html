Lucky Lunch

The Lucky Lunch is truly one of the most special foods you can have in Pelican Town. Legends say that one bite is enough to make your entire day special.

Ingredients

  • Sea Cucumber
  • Tortilla
  • Blue Jazz

Steps for Making!

  1. Heat up the tortilla.
  2. Cook and carve the cucumber into little stars!
  3. Use the mystical powers of the blue jazz flower to decorate and plate the dish!
  4. Enjoy before some big occasions to have a great boost of luck!
